Japanese Sukiyaki

Sweet and savory Japanese hot pot soup base

Description

A traditional Japanese broth featuring a balance of savory soy sauce and subtle sweetness. Often compared to the flavor of sukiyaki beef, this soup base is rich, comforting, and slightly sweet with deep umami flavor.

Allergen Information

• Soy
• Wheat
• Sesame (from Pork Bone White Soup base)
