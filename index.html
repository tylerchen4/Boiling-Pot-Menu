All You Can Eat

Rules

By ordering All You Can Eat, guests agree to follow all listed rules.

01
All you can eat is limited to 90 minutes per table.
02
All guests at the table must choose the same level: Regular or Premium.
03
Guests will be charged by the stated size of the party.
04
A 15% gratuity will be added to parties of 6 or more. Payments with gratuity must be made using one payment type only.
05
A maximum of 2 credit cards may be used per table.
06
Any leftovers will incur a $7 additional charge per person.
07
No take-out/to-go boxes can be provided for leftover food.
08
Dinner pricing applies all day on weekends, Saturday/Sunday, and holidays.
